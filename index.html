Research

A simulation model of Fast Cut Back of Power Plant based on MATLAB

This research establishes a simulation model for the steam heat balance of boiler superheater and high pressure bypass based on MATLAB to studied the main steam pressure and temperature characteristics. It can provide reference for the design of PCV valve and safety valve. Next we will build some simulation models for thermal performance prediction of reheater, deaerator, condenser, small turbine under FCB condition.


Zero Discharge of Desulfurization Wastewater in Laiwu Power Plant

In this research, I assist my professor to build the pilot test in Shandong Laiwu power plant. We also collect samples to study on the determination of ion content by Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometer and test the corrosion resistance of different metal materials to desulfurization wastewater.





Development of Power Plant Simulation Software

In this research, we develop a power plant simulation software called CALING by C# languoge. On this platform we build the thermal balance model of the thermal equipment of the power plant based on the iterative calculation and interpolation calculation. The comparison with historical data shows the correctness of the model. Then we develope a real-time calculation function, and calculate the real-time thermal economy indicators to point out the optimal operating conditions.





Numerical simulation and flow field optimization of spray tower for wet desulfurization

In this research, we use the computational fluid dynamics and GAMBIT to build the spray tower. After study the influence of the angle and shape of spray tower's baffle on desulfurization efficiency by fluent, we choose porous trays. Then analyz the influence of flue gas inlet angle on desulfurization efficiency.
