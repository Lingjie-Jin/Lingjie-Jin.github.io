Research

A Simulation Model of Fast Cut Back of a Power Plant Based on MATLAB

In this research, a simulation model was established for the steam heat balance of boiler superheater and high pressure bypass based on MATLAB to studied the main steam pressure and temperature characteristics. It can provide reference for the design of PCV valve and safety valve. In the future, I will build some simulation models for thermal performance prediction of reheater, deaerator, condenser, and small turbine under FCB condition.


Zero Discharge of Desulfurization Wastewater in Laiwu Power Plant

In this research, I assisted professor in building the pilot test in Shandong Laiwu power plant. I also collect the wastewater samples to study the determination of ion content by Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometer and tested the corrosion resistance of different metal materials to desulfurization wastewater.





Development of Power Plant Simulation Software

In this research, I developed a power plant simulation software called CALING by C# language. On this platform I built the thermal balance model of the thermal equipment of the power plant based on the iterative calculation and interpolation calculation. The comparison of historical data and simulation data shows the correctness of the model. Then developed a real-time calculation function, and calculated the real-time thermal economy indicators to point out the optimal operating conditions.





Numerical Simulation and Flow Field Optimization of Spray Tower for Wet Desulfurization

In this research, I used the computational fluid dynamics and GAMBIT to build the spray tower model. After studying the influence of the angle and shape of spray tower's baffle on desulfurization efficiency by FLUNET, porous trays was chosen as optimal choice. Then I analyzed the influence of flue gas inlet angle on desulfurization efficiency. The greater the angle from 0° to 15 °, the higher the desulfurization efficiency.
